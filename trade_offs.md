# Trade Offs

This note demonstrates the benefits of balance in life. In this context, balance is not a symptom of mediocrity, but it's a skill that is learned and practiced for tuning the efforts for values and ends.


## Kindness to Self

Despite what it seems, the argument for promoting the kindness to self is more straight forward compared to the argument against it. Also, the promotion argument is not based on the "Seeking Happiness, not success" line of thinking.

### Why you should be more kind to yourself?

The line of argument here is very personal to the author but it's likely going to resonate with other people dealing with idealism and self-loathing. Here, the base point is the intuitive and general idea that **doing hard things (DHT) is good**. Arguments for this base point are outside the scope of this section, so we only work with the related implications of this idea. The next step is to connect this idea to the issue of **having basic ambitions (HBA)**. Before getting to the connection between DHT and HBA, let's review some instances of HBA below. These are mostly ordinary activities, and nothing is wrong with them. They become an issue once someone have them as goals or ambitions, and waste considerable time and resources on them. Every instance is brought along side of a mainstream tool that's used for over-indulgment and a simple anti-dote.

1. **Food**. As a necessity for every living organism, everyone puts some level of effort for obtaining it. The issue is the famous "living for eating as oppose to eating for leaving". One mainstream tool is ordering apps like Uber Eats where you have easy access to the most palatable items fast, without the need for cooking effort or planning. You get what you desire almost immidiately. The anti-dote is the combination of fasting and meal-preap.

2. **Entertainment**. Opposite to food this one is not necessary at all because no one has ever really died of boredom. The mainstream tools are any infinite feed that you can doom-scroll on (e.g. YouTube shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok). I'm still struggling to find a cursory anti-dote but deactivating social media and black-and-white phone screen helps.

3. **Lust**. The essence of life. Multi-billion dollar industries are main streaming its access via promoting the hook-up culture and its psudo-access via porn. The anti-dote is striving for a meaningful relation with a romantic partner.

DHT and HBA have two-fold relation as:

1. Overcoming HBA via antidotes is a DHT.
2. Moving beyond HBAs leads to more sophisticated goals and persuing them requires a lot of DHTs.

In summary, you need to DHT to avoid HBA. 

On the other hand, avoiding HBAs increases your capacity for DHT. That's where kindness to self enters the picture. For DHT you need mental energy, the journey is meaningfull and full of hicups. If you keep bashing yourself for falling short or slipping away, you will waste your mental energy. Idealism sucks the meaning out of the steps of the journey.

In conclusion, in the path of persuing your values, be kind to yourself so you can sustain doing the right things. Doing the easy right things are trivial so you need to be capable of doing the hard things. Doing the hard things keeps you out of the valley of basic ambitions.
